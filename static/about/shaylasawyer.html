Shayla Sawyer

Dr. Shayla Sawyer is an Associate Professor in the Electrical, Computer & Systems Engineering Department at RPI and is a co-PI on the C-STEM grant. She holds a BS, Electrical Engineering from Hampton University, 2003 and a PhD in Electrical Engineering from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, 2006.
