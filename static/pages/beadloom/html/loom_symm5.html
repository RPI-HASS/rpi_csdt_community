Finally, we come to the example of the bead loom. Like many other Native American knowledge systems, the bead loom is much like a Cartesian coordinate system. We have beads in rows and columns: which is the X axis and which is the Y axis? If Europeans had never come to North America, what names would we use for "X axis" and "Y axis"?
